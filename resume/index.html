Resume

Education

  • Doctor of Philosophy in Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, 2020-2025.
    • Advisor: Prof. Chandra Nair
    • Research interests: Network Information Theory, Information Inequality, Non-convex Optimization, Combinatorics
    • Investigate relationship between inequalities in additive combinatorics and information theory.
    • Develop tools for solving families of non-convex optimization problems involving auxiliary random variables and upper concave envelopes.
    • Show uniqueness of local maximizers and develop efficient algorithms for solving certain non-convex optimization problems in information theory.
  • Bachelor of Science in Mathematics and Information Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong, 2016-2020.
